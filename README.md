## 🧑‍💻 About Me

I'm a **full-stack developer from Delhi 🇮🇳** who lives at the intersection of **AI and code**.

I don't just build *with* AI — I build *for* real people, ship fast, and document the journey loudly.  
Every day I'm pushing **5+ commits**, posting on **[LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/jayant-olhyan-5057292a1/)**, and somewhere in between, competing or judging at hackathons.

A few things that define how I work:

- 🤖 **AI-first everything** — Cursor, Copilot, Gemini, Claude, ChatGPT. AI is a core part of my dev stack, not a shortcut
- 🏆 **Hackathon native** — Team **Hack Homies**, multiple wins, and a **panelist at 20+ hackathons** across India
- 🔁 **Consistent by design** — 5 GitHub commits/day & a daily LinkedIn post aren't habits, they're non-negotiables
- 🇮🇳 **Building for India** — Hindi-first UIs, Indian user personas, India-specific APIs
- 💬 **I share everything** — wins, failures, process. If I built it, it's probably on my LinkedIn
- 📄 **Research too** — Presented at MRIIRS International Conference (March 2026) on AI-powered health systems

I'm strongest in **backend systems, APIs, and databases**, always leveling up on design,  
and permanently convinced the best ideas come from 48-hour hackathon sprints.
